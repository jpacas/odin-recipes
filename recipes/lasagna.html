Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Lasagna sauce
  • Lasagna cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles
  2. Mix the lasagna sauce and the lasagna cheese
  3. Layer the lasagna noodles, lasagna sauce, and lasagna cheese
  4. Bake the lasagna at 375 degrees for 25 minutes
