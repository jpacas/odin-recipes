Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Rice

Steps to make Tenessee Onions

  1. Slice the onions and cook them in a pan
  2. Add oil to the onions and cook them for 5 minutes
  3. Add salt and pepper to the onions and cook them for 10 minutes
  4. Add garlic to the onions and cook them for 5 minutes
  5. Serve the onions with a side of rice
