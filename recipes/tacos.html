Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Tortillas
  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream

Steps to make Tacos

  1. Cook the meat and vegetables
  2. Warm the tortillas
  3. Fill the tortillas with the meat, vegetables, onions, cheese, salsa, guacamole, and sour cream
  4. Serve the tacos with the cheese, salsa, guacamole, and sour cream
